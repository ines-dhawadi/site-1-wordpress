== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `team-builder` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to Tea Builder Panels menu to customize this plugin.
4. Copy your publish Team shortcode and then paste into your page or post to display it.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I Add Unlimited Team Members with help of this team plugin? =

Yes, you can add Unlimited Team Members help of this plguin , you can easily change their layout position through drag and drop them. 

= Can I able to display Teams on any post or page?  =

Yes, you can , with using team shortcode  [TEAM_B id=xx] (here xx your shortcode id) you can display your teams on any page or posts.

= Can i apply my own design css on my team members output  =

Using custom css you can apply your own custom design on frontend.

= Is this support social profiles for team members =

Yes, you can easily add your team members social profiles - Facebook, twitter linked-in and Google+ 

= How to add Team in widget area ?  =  

Just open your widget menu then add "Text widget" , now paste your Team shortcode [TEAM_B id=xx] (here xx your shortcode id) in this text widget.
